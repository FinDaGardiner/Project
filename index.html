Welcome to the Edinburgh Walking Tours Guide.


How to use

If you are using a mobile device, click on the burger menu in the top left and click on 'Tours', from then you can choose a tour to use which will guide you through the city to see the best sites it can offer.


If you do not have this, click on 'Tours' at the top of the screen


A review of this page would mean a lot and help improve this website, so after the tour if you could follow the link in the 'Reviews' section and give feedback that would be greatly appreciated!
